Terms & Conditions

1. Admission & Deposit

  • Every resident must submit a valid government ID proof at the time of admission.
  • A caution deposit of ₹5,000 must be paid at the time of joining. This is refundable at the time of leaving after room inspection.
  • Advance rent for one month is mandatory during admission.

2. Rent & Maintenance

  • Monthly rent must be paid before the 5th of every month.
  • A late fee of ₹100/day will be charged after the due date.
  • Maintenance fee of ₹500/month includes cleaning, repairs, and water charges.

3. Room Rules

  • Keep your room and common areas clean and tidy.
  • Damaging property or misuse of electric appliances will lead to a penalty or expulsion.
  • Cooking inside rooms is strictly prohibited.

4. Guest Policy

  • Visitors are allowed only during visiting hours: 9 AM to 7 PM.
  • Overnight stay of guests is strictly not allowed without prior written permission.

5. General Rules

  • Alcohol, smoking, and illegal substances are strictly prohibited.
  • Residents must return by 10:00 PM daily.
  • Management has the right to terminate stay for repeated violations.

Note: By enrolling, you agree to abide by these rules. Violation may result in fines or cancellation of accommodation without refund.
